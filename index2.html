Featured Players

Rohit Sharma

Indian Cricketer, Captain of the Indian Cricket Team in ODIs and T20Is, known for his explosive batting and world record of three double centuries in ODIs.

Virat Kohli

Indian Cricketer, Former Captain, widely regarded as one of the best batsmen in the world, known for his consistency and aggressive play style.

MS Dhoni

Former Indian Cricketer and Captain, known for his calm demeanor and incredible leadership, leading India to multiple ICC trophies.

Sachin Tendulkar

Legendary Indian Cricketer, often referred to as the "Little Master" or "Master Blaster," holds the record for the most runs in international cricket and numerous other records.

Yuvraj Singh

Former Indian Cricketer, known for his explosive batting and remarkable six sixes in an over during the 2007 T20 World Cup, played a key role in India's 2011 World Cup win.

Sourav Ganguly

Former Indian Captain and Cricketer, known for his leadership and aggressive style, played a significant role in building a competitive Indian team during his captaincy era.

Rahul Dravid

Former Indian Cricketer, often called "The Wall" for his solid defense and dependable batting, known for his technique and ability to play long innings.

Jasprit Bumrah

Indian Cricketer, renowned for his unique bowling action and ability to deliver crucial breakthroughs, one of the top bowlers in modern-day cricket.
